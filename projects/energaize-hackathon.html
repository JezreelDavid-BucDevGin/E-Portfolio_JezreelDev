// CHALLENGE

The Problem & Goal

Problem Statement

Energy consumption is a major concern for businesses and households. Traditional approaches lack real-time insights and predictive capabilities. Companies need intelligent systems to optimize energy usage, reduce waste, and lower costs while supporting sustainability goals.

Our Goal

Develop an AI-powered platform that analyzes energy consumption patterns, predicts future usage, and provides actionable recommendations for optimization within a 48-hour hackathon timeframe.

Key Learnings & Challenges

Challenge: Time Pressure

48-hour constraint required rapid decision-making, efficient team coordination, and prioritizing core features over perfection.

Learning: Agile Teamwork

Clear communication and role division among team members were crucial for delivering a complete solution under tight time constraints.

Challenge: Real-World Data

Working with actual Meralco energy data revealed the importance of robust data handling and validation in real-world ML applications.

Learning: MVP Thinking

Learned the value of building minimum viable products quickly and iterating, rather than trying to achieve perfection upfront.
